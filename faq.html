FAQ'S

Sounds too good to be true? Check out the questions
others had to answer the questions you have!




How much does it cost?

It's free!

How can I trust that the volunteers are safe to come and work in my classroom?

Every volunteer allows Lend-a-Hand to preform a background check ensuring the safety of the teachers and students.

How do I know when there is someone nearby who wants to volunteer in my class?

Lend-a-Hand notifies teachers when a volunteer matches their criteria in their area. Teachers will also receive a notification whenever they receive a personal message from a volunteer asking for more information about their class.

As a volunteer do I need to have past experience with children?

It is not necessary, but it is prefered by many teachers that their volunteers have experience working with students prior to working in their classroom.

How long are the volunteers available?

That is up to the volunteers. They are available for as long as they want to help with your class.
